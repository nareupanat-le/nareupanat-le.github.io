Nareupanat Lekkoksung

Associate Professor in Mathematics, Division of Mathematics
RMUTI (Khon Kaen Campus)

I am an Associate Professor in mathematics at the Division of Mathematics, Rajamangala University of Technology Isan, Khon Kaen Campus. My research interests are the theory of (fuzzy) ordered semigroups and their generalizations, and the algebraic properties of hypersubstitutions.
